About me

I received the B.S. degree from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2020. Now, I am a PhD student in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University since 2020. My main research interests include deep reinforcement learning (DRL), intelligent decision-making, intelligent scheduling, autonomous driving.

During my PhD studies, I have published over 30 papers in SCI journals and international conferences such as IEEE T-ITS, IEEE T-IV, TRC, and IEEE ITSC. I have been cited over 500 times in Google Scholar and have applied for 7 national invention patents. I have also been invited to serve as a reviewer for multiple SCI journals and international conferences.

I was awarded the honors of Beijing Outstanding Graduate, Outstanding Graduate Student of Beijing Automation Society, National Scholarship for Doctoral Students, Tsinghua University First Class Scholarship for Overall Excellence, Tsinghua University Zheng Weimin Scholarship, and so on.
