About me

I received the B.S. degree from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2020. Now, I am a PhD student in the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University since 2020. My main research interests include deep reinforcement learning (DRL), intelligent decision-making, intelligent scheduling, autonomous driving.

During my PhD studies, I have published over 30 papers in SCI journals and international conferences such as IEEE T-ITS, IEEE T-IV, TRC, and IEEE ITSC. I have been cited over 500 in Google Scholar and have applied for 7 invention patents. I have also been invited to serve as a reviewer for several SCI journals and international conferences.

I was awarded the honors of Outstanding Graduate of Beijing [北京市优秀毕业生], Outstanding Graduate Student of BEIJING ASSOCIATION OF AUTOMATION [北京自动化学会优秀研究生(北京市每年约10人)], National Scholarship [国家奖学金，Top 2% in Tsinghua University], Excellent Comprehensive Scholarship of Tsinghua University [清华大学综合优秀奖学金（一等）] , and so on.
